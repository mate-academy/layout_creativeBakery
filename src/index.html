about us

Like many design teams, Onfido made the transition from Abstract + Sketch, to Figma in the last couple of years. One of the common resistance points internally bevore making that switch, was Figma's lack of Git-style branching that Abstract enabled. The design team had come to rely on branching and had built a lot of our processes around it.
